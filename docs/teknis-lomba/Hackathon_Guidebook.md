
# Hackathon Guidebook

## Competition Description

### Description
The Hackathon is an innovation competition themed around Industry 4.0 technologies, designed to foster the development of creative and applicable solutions to challenges in modern mining operations. The competition provides a platform to explore ideas, build system prototypes, and create digital products that enhance efficiency, safety, productivity, and sustainability in the mining industry.

The event is open to students, professionals, and the general public with an interest in technology and mining. Each team consists of 3–5 members with interdisciplinary expertise such as programming, data analytics, system design, geotechnics, and mining operations management.

After an initial evaluation phase, the top teams advance to the final round to present innovations directly to industry practitioners and professionals at ISMC XV.

### Purpose
- Stimulate technological innovation in mining using Industry 4.0 concepts  
- Produce applicable solutions for efficiency, safety, productivity, and sustainability  
- Provide a collaborative platform for problem solving  
- Expand understanding of modern mining dynamics  
- Enable real-world implementation opportunities  

### Target
- General public  
- Exclusively Indonesian citizens

## General Regulations

### Participants Regulations
- Open to Indonesian citizens  
- Teams of 3–5 members  
- One team per participant  
- One project per team  
- No changes to team composition after registration  

### General Rules
- Original work only, no plagiarism  
- Copyright belongs to participants  
- Asynchronous participation  
- Judges’ decisions are final  
- Registration fee is non-refundable  
- Official name on certificate follows website registration  

## Competition Mechanism

### Registration
- Register via ismc-xv.com  
- Submit team and member information  

### Innovation Proposal Submission
- PDF format, max 10 MB  
- Naming format: Description_Team Name_Subtheme_Project Title.pdf  
- Turnitin similarity max 20%  

### Program Development Stage – Full Submission
- Demo video (max 20 minutes) uploaded to YouTube (unlisted)  
- Source files submitted to committee  
- Late submission penalty applies  

### Final Presentation Stage
- Top 5 teams  
- 10-minute presentation + 15-minute Q&A  
- Live demo at Institut Teknologi Bandung

### Innovation Proposal
